Pier Giuseppe Sessa

Ph.D. candidate @ ETH Zürich.
 
  LinkedIn

I am a doctoral candidate at ETH Zürich, under the supervision of Prof. Maryam Kamgarpour (Automatic Control Laboratory) and Prof. Andreas Krause (Institute for Machine Learning). My research focuses on multi-agent learning systems, online learning, and sequential decision-making.

During my PhD, I have interned with Google Brain in 2022 and Google Research in 2021. Prior, I was an intern at the Institute for Transport Planning and Systems of ETH Zürich in 2018, and the United Technologies Research Center (Cork, Ireland) in 2016. At ETH, I have been Teaching Assistant of the Master's level courses Control Systems II (Head TA) and System Identification during 2018-2021. I obtained a MSc. in Robotics, Systems and Control from ETH Zürich (2017) and a BSc. in Automation Engineering from Politecnico di Milano (2015), both with honors.
